Discover Cameroon

From mountains and rainforests to savannahs and beaches — explore it all.

Mount Cameroon

Known locally as Fako, this active volcano offers challenging hikes and sweeping coastal views.

  • Best time: December–March
  • Activities: Hiking, volcano festival
  • Nearby: Buea, Limbe

Waza National Park

One of Cameroon’s premier wildlife reserves, home to elephants, lions, and giraffes.

  • Best time: November–April
  • Activities: Safari, birdwatching
  • Nearby: Maroua

Limbe Beaches

Volcanic black sands, botanical gardens, and a laid-back coastal vibe.

  • Best time: November–March
  • Activities: Beach, seafood, gardens
  • Nearby: Buea, Idenau

Bamenda Highlands

Rolling hills, craft markets, and cultural heritage sites across the Northwest region.

  • Best time: October–March
  • Activities: Hiking, culture, crafts
  • Nearby: Bamenda
