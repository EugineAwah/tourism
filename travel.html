Plan your trip

Visa guidance, transport options, and safety tips for travelling in Cameroon.

Air travel

International gateways: Douala International Airport (DLA) and Yaoundé Nsimalen International Airport (NSI). Regional airports connect major cities.

  • Tip: Check seasonal schedules and weather conditions.
  • Local: Domestic routes are limited; plan ground transfers.

Road travel

Highways link major cities; rural roads can be challenging during rainy seasons.

  • Tip: Use reputable bus lines or private drivers.
  • Safety: Day travel preferred for visibility.

Rail travel

Camrail line from Douala to Ngaoundéré offers long-distance travel across regions.

  • Tip: Book in advance; verify schedules.
  • Comfort: Choose sleeper options for overnight routes.

Visa & safety

Visitors typically require a visa; consult official consular guidance. Prioritize health precautions and local advisories.

  • Docs: Passport validity and visa requirements.
  • Health: Vaccinations and travel insurance recommended.
  • Local: Respect cultural norms and seek local guidance.
